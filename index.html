Cairo, Egypt
I have worked as a night manager in Cairo. But soon my whole life changed due to my loyalty to the Queen and because of another woman who stole some documents and my heart too.
Our short and sweet romance ran into a dark and bloody ending soon so I felt that I had to left Cairo.
I returned several years later, when I had the possibility to "complete all my unfinished projects".
And I can tell, I did it very well...

Zermatt, Switzerland
In Switzerland I have been working again as a night manager at a remote hotel. I just wanted to hide away from the world, but my destiny found me when Mr. Roper and his company arrived. That occasion drove me to volunteere for a task.
I met here Mrs. Burr first, she was my very best menthor. But on her "influence" I had to leave my whole past behind...
3 / 3
Mallorca, Spain
In Maillorca first I've worked as a cook in a small seaside restaurant, but "all of a sudden" and in a "suprisingly unexpected way" I found myself in a very different situation due to a heroic action, when I saved Mr. Roper's son from kidnappers.
After I recovered from my injuries I got very wide range of responsibilities here. First I just looked after the son of Mr. Roper but soon he discoveres my hidden skills and I became his partner in business, made a weapon demostration for his foreign partners.
I did here a few things that isn't make me proud, but got some very important experience...
